## Decisions Made

- **Relocation (REVIEWS.md H1):** CONTEXT.md D-05 specified `crates/roz-test/src/mock_provider.rs`. Relocated to `crates/roz-agent/src/model/mock_provider.rs`. Evidence: `roz-agent/Cargo.toml:55` declares `roz-test = { path = "../roz-test" }` as a dev-dep; adding `roz-agent` as a dep of `roz-test` would cycle under `cargo check --all-targets`.
