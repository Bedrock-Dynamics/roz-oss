- **Extended `spawn_with_policy_and_io` signature** instead of adding a parallel `*_with_latch_persist` entry. One production caller (`crates/roz-worker/src/main.rs:754`); doubling the entry surface would create two paths to maintain. Extending keeps path count at 1.

- **Helper-module extraction** for the subscriber spawn loops.
